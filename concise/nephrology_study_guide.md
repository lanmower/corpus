## Study Strategy for Nephrology Mastery

### Foundation Phase
1. **Nephrology Introduction** - Start here to understand kidney anatomy, physiology, and the clinical syndromes you'll study
2. **Acute Kidney Disease** - Learn the acute presentations, classification (KDIGO), and immediate management
3. **Approach To Chronic Kidney Disease** - Understand CKD staging, pathogenesis, and long-term management

### Clinical Complications Phase
4. **Diabetic Kidney Disease** - The most common cause of CKD globally; learn pathophysiology and management
5. **Glomerular Disease** - Primary and secondary glomerulonephritis; nephrotic vs nephritic syndromes
6. **Hypertension & The Kidney** - Bidirectional relationship: HTN causes kidney disease AND kidney disease causes HTN
7. **HIV & The Kidney** - HIV-associated nephropathy, immune complex disease, drug nephrotoxicity

### Specialized Topics Phase
8. **Urinary Tract Infections (UTI)** - Distinguish upper vs lower UTI; management by infection type
9. **Drugs & The Kidney** - Critical safety: nephrotoxic agents, dosing adjustments, contrast nephropathy
10. **Kidney Replacement Therapy** - Dialysis modalities, transplantation, and end-stage renal disease management

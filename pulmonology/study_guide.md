### Foundation
1. **Read sequentially**: Start with Community-Acquired Pneumonia (epidemiology, diagnosis, management), then Lung Cancer (etiology, diagnosis, presentation), then Antibiotic Stewardship (drug selection), and finally Suppurative Lung Syndromes (bronchiectasis, abscess, empyema).
2. **Extract key definitions**: Note clinical definitions, diagnostic criteria, and evidence-based treatment protocols.
3. **Map relationships**: Understand how infections, risk factors, and comorbidities influence clinical presentation and management.

### Active Learning
1. **Review by topic**: Create summaries for pneumonia management, cancer screening and diagnosis, antibiotic selection principles, and suppurative conditions.
2. **Clinical reasoning**: For each condition, trace from epidemiology → risk factors → pathology → clinical presentation → diagnosis → management.
3. **Practice application**: Consider how concepts apply to patient scenarios with different comorbidities (HIV, TB, age, immune status).

### Mastery Checklist
- [ ] Define community-acquired pneumonia, risk factors, and epidemiology in South African context
- [ ] Explain diagnostic approaches and severity scoring (CURB)
- [ ] Know empiric antibiotic selection based on patient age, comorbidities, and antibiotic exposure
- [ ] Understand lung cancer etiology, screening, and clinical features
- [ ] Explain paraneoplastic syndromes and their pathophysiology
- [ ] Know antibiotic mechanisms, resistance patterns, and stewardship principles
- [ ] Define bronchiectasis, lung abscess, and empyema with their etiologies
- [ ] Understand management principles for suppurative lung conditions
- [ ] Apply knowledge to clinical scenarios with multiple comorbidities

### Key Topics Summary
- **Epidemiology & Risk**: HIV prevalence impact, smoking-related risk, healthcare burden
- **Diagnosis**: Chest radiography, severity assessment, organism identification
- **Treatment**: Evidence-based empiric therapy, de-escalation strategies, stewardship
- **Complications**: Metastatic disease, paraneoplastic syndromes, resistant infections
- **Prevention**: Vaccination, screening, smoking cessation

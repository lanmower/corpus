High Risk
History
Angina at rest in the preceding 48 hours
Examination
Haemodynamic instability
Tachycardia, bradycardia, hypotension
Impaired LV function: S3, crackles, pulmonary oedema, EF < 40%
Advanced elderly > 75 years
ECG
ST depression or transient elevation
Bundle branch block or tachyarrhythmias
Biomarkers
Troponin elevation

Transient ST-segment elevation

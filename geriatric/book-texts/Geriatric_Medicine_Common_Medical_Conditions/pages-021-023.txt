Factors that predispose older adults to UTI
Use of catheters (80% of UTI in hospitalized patients)
Neurogenic bladder
Enlarged prostate
Increase in vaginal pH
Vaginal atrophy
Incomplete bladder emptying

--- page 22 ---
Respiratory infections in the older adult
Reasons for increased susceptibility to pneumonia
Changes in pulmonary reserve
Decreased mucociliary transport
Decreased cough reflex
Decreased elasticity of alveoli
Poorer ventilation

--- page 23 ---
Influenza
80-90% of deaths due to influenza occur in adults ≥ 65 years
Older adults are prone to severe complications due to co-existing chronic diseases and weakened immunity
Benefit most from vaccination, early detection and aggressive therapy

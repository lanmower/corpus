--- page 59 ---
Definition
NSTE ACS versus Unstable Angina
Differ only in severity
NSTE ACS
The ischaemia is severe enough to cause myocardial necrosis and release of detectable quantities of a marker of myocardial injury (troponin +)
Unstable Angina
No biochemical marker of myocardial necrosis is detectable (troponin -)

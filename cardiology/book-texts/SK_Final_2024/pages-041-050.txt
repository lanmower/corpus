MANAGEMENT OF STEMI
Chest pain > 30 minutes
+
ECG  STE  >  1 mm in limb leads
>  2 mm in precordial lead
(or new  LBBB)
REPERFUSION
[NOTES]

--- page 48 ---
Management of STEMI : Reperfusion
Primary PCI (angioplasty/stenting) best therapy
Fibrinolysis if onset of pain < 12 hours
Streptokinase
1.5 million units as an infusion over one hour
Alteplase
100 mg total dose infused over 1.5 hr
15 mg IV bolus over 1-2 minutes,
then 50 mg IV infusion over next 30 minutes
then remaining 35 mg over next 60 minutes
Tenectaplase
0.53 mg/kg IV bolus over 5 seconds

--- page 49 ---
Anti-thrombotic Therapy in STEMI
Anti-platelet
Aspirin 325mg to chew then 100mg/d per os indefinitely
Clopidogrel 300mg stat, then 75 mg/d per os for at least 1 year
Anti-thrombins
Enoxaparin 30 mg IVI stat, then 1 mg/kg subcut 12 hourly
OR
Heparin 60 IU/kg stat (not > 4000 IU), then 12 IU/kg IV hourly infusion (not > 1000 IU/hour) for 48 hrs.

Sinus tachycardia
Sinus rhythm with heart rate 100-150/min
Causes
Physiological (anxiety, pain, fever, exercise)
Drugs, eg. Adrenaline, atropine, caffeine, alcohol, salbutamol
Anaemia
Hyperthyroidism
Hypovolaemia
